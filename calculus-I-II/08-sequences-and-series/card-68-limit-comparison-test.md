# Card 68: Limit Comparison Test

**Section:** Sequences and Series

## Front

What is the Limit Comparison Test?

## Back

For positive series $\sum a_n$ and $\sum b_n$, compute:

$$\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{a_n}{b_n}=c$$

If $0<c<\infty$, then $\sum a_n$ and $\sum b_n$ either both converge or both diverge.

Special cases:

- If $c=0$ and $\sum b_n$ converges, then $\sum a_n$ converges.
- If $c=\infty$ and $\sum b_n$ diverges, then $\sum a_n$ diverges.
