# Card 66: Integral Test

**Section:** Sequences and Series

## Front

When can you use the Integral Test?

## Back

If $f(x)$ is:

- Positive
- Continuous
- Decreasing

and $a_n=f(n)$, then:

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}a_n\qquad\text{and}\qquad\int_1^\infty f(x)\,dx$$

either both converge or both diverge.
