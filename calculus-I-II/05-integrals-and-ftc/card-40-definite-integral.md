# Card 40: Definite integral

**Section:** Integrals and the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

## Front

What does a definite integral represent?

## Back

$$\int_a^b f(x)\,dx$$

represents the signed area between $f(x)$ and the $x$-axis from $x=a$ to $x=b$.

- Area above the $x$-axis is positive.
- Area below the $x$-axis is negative.
