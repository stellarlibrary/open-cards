# Card 4: Logarithm rules

**Section:** Precalculus Review

## Front

What logarithm rules are most useful in calculus?

## Back

Key rules:

$$\ln(ab)=\ln a+\ln b$$

$$\ln\!\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)=\ln a-\ln b$$

$$\ln(a^r)=r\ln a$$

$$\ln(e^x)=x$$

$$e^{\ln x}=x$$

These are especially useful for logarithmic differentiation and integration.
