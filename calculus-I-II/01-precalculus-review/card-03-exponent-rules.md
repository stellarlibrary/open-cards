# Card 3: Exponent rules

**Section:** Precalculus Review

## Front

What exponent rules are most useful in calculus?

## Back

Key rules:

$$a^m a^n = a^{m+n}$$

$$\frac{a^m}{a^n}=a^{m-n}$$

$$(a^m)^n=a^{mn}$$

$$a^{-n}=\frac{1}{a^n}$$

$$a^{1/n}=\sqrt[n]{a}$$

Useful for rewriting before differentiating or integrating.
