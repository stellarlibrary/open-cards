# Card 7: Direct substitution

**Section:** Limits and Continuity

## Front

When can you evaluate a limit by direct substitution?

## Back

If $f$ is continuous at $a$, then:

$$\lim_{x\to a} f(x)=f(a)$$

Common continuous functions include:

- Polynomials
- Exponential functions
- Logarithms on their domain
- Trig functions on their domain
- Rational functions where denominator is not zero
