# Card 9: Important trig limit

**Section:** Limits and Continuity

## Front

What is the fundamental trigonometric limit?

## Back

$$\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=1$$

Related limits:

$$\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\tan x}{x}=1$$

$$\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{1-\cos x}{x}=0$$

This limit is important for deriving derivatives of trigonometric functions.
