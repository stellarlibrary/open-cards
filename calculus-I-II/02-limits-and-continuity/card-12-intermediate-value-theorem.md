# Card 12: Intermediate Value Theorem

**Section:** Limits and Continuity

## Front

What does the Intermediate Value Theorem say?

## Back

If $f$ is continuous on $[a,b]$, and $N$ is between $f(a)$ and $f(b)$, then there exists some $c\in(a,b)$ such that:

$$f(c)=N$$

Common use: proving that an equation has a solution.
