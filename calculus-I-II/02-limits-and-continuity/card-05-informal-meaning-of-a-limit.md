# Card 5: Informal meaning of a limit

**Section:** Limits and Continuity

## Front

What does $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a} f(x)=L$ mean?

## Back

It means that as $x$ gets closer and closer to $a$, the function values $f(x)$ get closer and closer to $L$.

The value of $f(a)$ does not need to equal $L$, and $f(a)$ does not even need to exist.
