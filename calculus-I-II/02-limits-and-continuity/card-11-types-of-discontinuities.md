# Card 11: Types of discontinuities

**Section:** Limits and Continuity

## Front

What are the main types of discontinuities?

## Back

Common types:

1. **Removable discontinuity** — a hole in the graph.
2. **Jump discontinuity** — left and right limits exist but are not equal.
3. **Infinite discontinuity** — function approaches $\infty$ or $-\infty$.
4. **Oscillating discontinuity** — function oscillates and has no limit.
