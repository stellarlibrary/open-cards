# Card 59: Surface area of revolution

**Section:** Applications of Integration

## Front

What is the surface area formula for rotation around the $x$-axis?

## Back

For $y=f(x)\ge 0$:

$$S=2\pi\int_a^b f(x)\sqrt{1+[f'(x)]^2}\,dx$$

Around the $y$-axis:

$$S=2\pi\int_a^b x\sqrt{1+[f'(x)]^2}\,dx$$
