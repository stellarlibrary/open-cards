# Card 33: Optimization problem strategy

**Section:** Applications of Derivatives

## Front

What is the general strategy for optimization problems?

## Back

1. Identify the quantity to maximize or minimize.
2. Write an objective function.
3. Use constraints to express the function in one variable.
4. Determine the domain.
5. Differentiate.
6. Find critical points.
7. Test endpoints and critical points.
8. Interpret the answer.
