# Card 30: Concavity

**Section:** Applications of Derivatives

## Front

How does the second derivative determine concavity?

## Back

If $f''(x)>0$, then the graph is concave up.

If $f''(x)<0$, then the graph is concave down.

Concave up means the graph bends like a cup. Concave down means the graph bends like a cap.
