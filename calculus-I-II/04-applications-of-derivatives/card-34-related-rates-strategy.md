# Card 34: Related rates strategy

**Section:** Applications of Derivatives

## Front

How do you solve related rates problems?

## Back

1. Draw a diagram.
2. Label variables.
3. Write an equation relating the variables.
4. Differentiate both sides with respect to time $t$.
5. Substitute known values after differentiating.
6. Solve for the desired rate.

Key idea: $\dfrac{d}{dt}$ must be applied because variables change over time.
