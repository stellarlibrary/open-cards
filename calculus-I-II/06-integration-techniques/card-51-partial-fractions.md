# Card 51: Partial fractions

**Section:** Integration Techniques

## Front

When should you use partial fraction decomposition?

## Back

Use partial fractions for rational functions:

$$\frac{P(x)}{Q(x)}$$

where $P$ and $Q$ are polynomials.

If degree of $P$ is greater than or equal to degree of $Q$, first use polynomial long division.

Example form:

$$\frac{1}{(x-1)(x+2)}=\frac{A}{x-1}+\frac{B}{x+2}$$
