# Card 49: Trig integrals: even powers

**Section:** Integration Techniques

## Front

How do you integrate even powers of sine and cosine?

## Back

Use power-reduction identities:

$$\sin^2 x=\frac{1-\cos 2x}{2}$$

$$\cos^2 x=\frac{1+\cos 2x}{2}$$

Useful for integrals like $\int \sin^2 x\,dx$ or $\int \cos^4 x\,dx$.
