# Card 47: Trig integrals: odd power of sine

**Section:** Integration Techniques

## Front

How do you integrate $\int \sin^m x \cos^n x\,dx$ when sine has an odd power?

## Back

If the power of sine is odd:

1. Save one $\sin x$.
2. Convert remaining sine powers using:

$$\sin^2 x=1-\cos^2 x$$

3. Let $u=\cos x$, because $du=-\sin x\,dx$.
