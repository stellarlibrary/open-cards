# Card 53: Convergence of p-integrals

**Section:** Integration Techniques

## Front

When does the $p$-integral converge?

## Back

For:

$$\int_1^\infty \frac{1}{x^p}\,dx$$

the integral converges if $p>1$ and diverges if $p\le 1$.

For:

$$\int_0^1 \frac{1}{x^p}\,dx$$

the integral converges if $p<1$ and diverges if $p\ge 1$.
