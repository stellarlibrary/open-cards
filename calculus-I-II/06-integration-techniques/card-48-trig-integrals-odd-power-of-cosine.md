# Card 48: Trig integrals: odd power of cosine

**Section:** Integration Techniques

## Front

How do you integrate $\int \sin^m x \cos^n x\,dx$ when cosine has an odd power?

## Back

If the power of cosine is odd:

1. Save one $\cos x$.
2. Convert remaining cosine powers using:

$$\cos^2 x=1-\sin^2 x$$

3. Let $u=\sin x$, because $du=\cos x\,dx$.
