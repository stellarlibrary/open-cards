# Card 24: Implicit differentiation

**Section:** Derivatives

## Front

When do you use implicit differentiation?

## Back

Use implicit differentiation when $y$ is not isolated as a function of $x$.

Example:

$$x^2+y^2=25$$

Differentiate both sides:

$$2x+2y\frac{dy}{dx}=0$$

Solve:

$$\frac{dy}{dx}=-\frac{x}{y}$$
