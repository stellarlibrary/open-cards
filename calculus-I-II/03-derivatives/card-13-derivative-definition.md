# Card 13: Derivative definition

**Section:** Derivatives

## Front

What is the derivative of $f(x)$ at $x=a$?

## Back

The derivative is:

$$f'(a)=\lim_{h\to 0}\frac{f(a+h)-f(a)}{h}$$

It represents:

- Instantaneous rate of change
- Slope of the tangent line
- Velocity if $f$ represents position
